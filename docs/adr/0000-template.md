## Context

What forces are at play — the technical, operational, or security constraints that make
this a real decision with non-obvious trade-offs. State the problem, not the solution.

## Decision

The position taken, in one or two sentences, plus the key specifics. Active voice:
"We use X." / "A device is owned by exactly one worker."

## Consequences

- **Positive:** what this buys us.
- **Negative / cost:** what it makes harder, and what we accept.
- **Follow-ups:** anything deferred or that this enables/blocks.

## Alternatives considered

Briefly, the options rejected and why — so the decision can be re-evaluated if the forces
change.
